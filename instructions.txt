User login form & dashboard responsive design - Mobile First:
-------------------------------------------------------------

Create Login form with Sign in and register buttons.
Two text boxes for username and password

register button opens register modal dialog box.
register modal occupies full screen with 50 - 70% opacity.

register box consists of:
-firstname, lastname, username, password & confirm password.
-register & cancel buttons. cancel button closes dialog.
-close button in top right corner, esc key and click on transparent part of modal also closes dialog.

after register users are able to login.
after login users are presented with a dashboard and welcome text.
